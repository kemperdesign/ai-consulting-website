Good Questions, Straight Answers

Things most people want to know before reaching out.

We'd rather answer these upfront than have you spend the first half of a call asking them.

🎯

Do I need to be technical?

No. We handle everything on the technical side. Our job is to make sure you understand what we're building and why — in plain language, not jargon. If something doesn't make sense, ask.

💰

How much does it cost?

Every project is different, so we won't give you a number before we understand what you need. After the discovery call, you'll receive a written proposal with a fixed price before any work starts.

⏱️

How quickly will I see results?

Most projects go live within 2–6 weeks. 90% of clients report a measurable ROI within 60 days. We track this with you — and tell you when something isn't performing as expected.

🔒

What happens to my business data?

Nothing leaves your hands without your knowledge. We never store, sell, or train on your data. Privacy is a hard requirement on every project — not an optional extra.

🔄

What if something doesn't work?

If any tool we build underperforms in the first 30 days after launch, we fix it at no extra cost. That's not a special guarantee — it's just how we operate.

📞

What does support look like after launch?

You'll have a named contact, a monthly check-in call, and direct access to us for questions. We don't disappear after launch — a growing business always has new things to work on.
